About the Festival: The Africa Summer Festival is a joyous annual event that invites you to immerse yourself in the vibrant tapestry of African culture. From pulsating rhythms to captivating art, tantalizing flavors, and engaging workshops, this festival is a sensory journey through the heart of Africa. Join us as we celebrate the rich diversity, warm hospitality, and cultural heritage that make Africa truly unique.
